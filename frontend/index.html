How this search works

What it does well
  • Finds relevant information from your organisation's documents
  • Summarises long documents into a direct answer
  • Shows which document and page the answer came from
  • Your data stays on this server
What to keep in mind
  • It can only answer from documents uploaded here
  • It does not use live internet knowledge
  • Always verify important answers in the cited source
  • If no source is shown, treat the answer carefully
Tips for better results
  • Be specific in your question
  • Ask one question at a time
  • If results seem off, rephrase with more context
